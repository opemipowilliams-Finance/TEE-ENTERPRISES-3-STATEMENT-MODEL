with operations beginning in **January 2028**.

## Assumptions
All key assumptions are documented separately and include:

- Revenue by product line (pricing, volume, growth rates)
- Cost of goods sold and direct cost inflation
- Fixed and variable operating expenses
- Working capital assumptions (AR, AP, inventory days)
- Capital expenditures and depreciation
- Debt financing, interest, and repayment schedule
- Corporate tax rate
